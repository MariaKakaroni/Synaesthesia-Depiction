Make your own synaesthesia depiction

Description

The purpose of this application is to depict the experience that a synesthete has. You can choose differnet types of well known geometrical shapes-curves, patterns, colors, sizes and create a HQ SVG file that can be downloaded threw the save button. The shapes-curves can be chosen by clicking on them on the SHAPES section (shapes table) of the page. The shape will be drawn on the CANVAS and you can then move-resize-recolor-refill-rotate it as you wish. You can add as many shapes as he/she wants. There are undo/redo buttons for erasing/redraw the last shape that was drawn. You can also create patterns on the SVG canvas. First you have to choose and modify (one at a time) every shape/curve that you want to exist on the canvas. Then you have to go on the patterns section and choose the number of the repetition that the pattern will have horizontally and vertically. Press the button and the result will appear on the canvas.
